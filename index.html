Common questions

Frequently asked questions

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

Walk-ins are welcome, but booking on WhatsApp or by phone means little to no waiting when you arrive.

Is root canal treatment painful?

Modern root canals are done under local anaesthesia and are far more comfortable than most people expect. Our patients regularly describe the experience as painless.

Do you treat children?

Yes — paediatric (children's) dentistry is one of our services, and we take extra care to make kids feel comfortable.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

Call us immediately at 098969 36360. We'll tell you what to do right away and see you as soon as possible.
